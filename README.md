# AR Business Card

AR Business Card is an **Augmented Reality (AR) project** that enhances a traditional business card by adding interactive digital content. When the physical card is scanned using a mobile device, augmented reality technology overlays digital elements such as 3D objects, contact information, and interactive visuals on top of the card.

The project demonstrates how **AR technology can transform traditional networking tools into engaging digital experiences**, making it easier to share information in a modern and interactive way.

## Features

* **Augmented Reality scanning** of a physical business card
* Display of a **3D model with rotating animation**
* **Clickable social media links** for quick access to profiles
* Interactive **contact information visualization**
* **Real-time AR overlay** on the business card
* **Enhanced UI** for better user experience

## Tech Stack

* **Unity** – Application development
* **Vuforia** – AR tracking and image recognition
* **C#** – Scripting and interaction logic

## Use Cases

* Modern **digital networking**
* **Interactive portfolios**
* **Marketing and branding**
* Demonstrating **AR technology applications**
